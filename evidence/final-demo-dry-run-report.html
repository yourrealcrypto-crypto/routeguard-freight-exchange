Fail-closed demonstrations

Wrong recipient → BLOCKED before signature — funds moved: 0

Payment verifier binds payTo to the winning carrier account. A mismatched recipient is rejected before signature / settlement.

Duplicate retry → cached result — settle calls: 1

Durable settle claim CAS ensures facilitator settle is invoked at most once per reservation attempt. Observed settleCallCount in this evidence: 1.

Failed settlement → no reservation, no ROUTE_RESERVED

ROUTE_RESERVED is published only after Mirror SUCCESS. Failed or inconclusive settlement cannot create an operational reservation.
