## Trademark constraints (observed)

- Do not modify, recolor, distort, or recreate Hedera marks.
- Do not make Hedera the most prominent brand on RouteGuard materials.
- Do not imply affiliation, sponsorship, or endorsement by Hedera Hashgraph, LLC.
- Always include the non-affiliation disclaimer when Hedera marks are displayed.
- Styrene fonts were **not** downloaded (licensed; excluded by policy).
- No third-party logo sites were used.
- The public “Hedera Logo Library” ZIP linked from brand.hedera.com returned HTTP 404 at retrieval; assets were taken only from the official brand.hedera.com asset CDN.

## Not included

- Styrene font files
- Hedera Council member logos
- Any recolored / rebuilt marks
